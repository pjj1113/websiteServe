How to choose a journal?

1.Whether the paper is within the scope of contribution.
2.Whether paper format complies with journal requirements.
3.Whether the paper fits journal preference.
You can make a list for option journals in line with paper theme, make comparisons for screening and finalize the one you would like to contribute. You can also choose an appropriate journal based on communications with PLOW Publishing.
